About Us

Who are We...

Our goal is to help your child grow mentally, physically, and emotionally by providing a safe, nurturing, and wholesome environment conducive to the full range of developmental needs.

We follow the Ministry Of Education method of teaching where children of mixed ages work together in the classroom doing age-appropriate activities.

Future Minds Liberia is led by Henrique W. Monger Jr. who holds a Diploma in Liberia, Child Psychology. He has been pursuing a career as a Liberian teacher for more than 5 years.
